How to use Mission Central CMS
📋Step by step
1
Choose a section from the sidebar
Select News, Events, or Employee Directory on the left. You can only edit sections your department owns.
2
Fill in your changes
Type your updated content into the form fields. You don't need to know any code — just type in plain English.
3
Click Submit for Approval
Your changes are sent to MarCom for review. You'll receive an email confirmation right away.
4
Changes go live
Once approved, your content appears on Mission Central automatically. You'll get an email when it's live.

Contact MarCom at support@ywcacolumbus.org for content questions. For access code issues, contact itsupport@ywcacolumbus.org.
